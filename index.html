3D Scanning

Fitsme Scan is a 3D scanning software that bridges the gap between 3D scanning apps for enthusiastic amateurs and professional solutions that involve expensive dedicated hardware.

Our Technology

Fitsme Scan allows creation of decent textured models using regular iPhones or any other cheap LIDAR-equipped cameras after minor modification.

The system consists of three parts:

1.


An iPhone application that collects raw scan data and sends it to a workstation.

2.


A portable program for processing raw scans and generating 3D models.

3.


A lightweight 3D-viewer for embedding into web pages.
